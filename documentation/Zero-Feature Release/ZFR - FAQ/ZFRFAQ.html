Accounts

How do I get an account?

1) Go to TeamPlayer: [LINK HERE]

2) Register with your information.

3) You are automatically created into a group with just you!

How can I invite my friends?

1) Click on the “Groups” tab.

2) Navigate to an existing group or create a new group.

3) Click “Add Member”. If they have an account, you can search for them by name or username. If not, you can invite them by email.

Tasks

How do I add a task?

1) Click on “Add Task” either from Home or from the Tasks tab.

2) Input name, description…

3) Select the group it will be for,  as well as the people who will be apart of it.

4) Cycle and/or set a deadline for the task.

What is a cycle?

1) Add multiple people from a group to a task, so that each one of them have a turn at a task.

2) During task creation, you can control the order of the cycle.

How do I edit a task?

1) Click on the task, a popup should appear.

2) Click “Edit”.

How do I mark a task as done?

1) Click on the task, a popup should appear.

2) Click “Finished”.

Bills

How do I add a bill?

1) Click on “Add Bill” from Home or the Bills tab.

2) Input name, description, total, and members who are apart of the bill.

3) Members names will automatically appear. Click to set their totals.

How can I edit a bill?

1) Click on the bill, a popup should appear.

2) Click “Edit”.

How can I pay a bill?

1) Click on the Bills tab and find the member you want to pay back.

2) Click on the bill, and a popup should appear.

3) Select or deselect bills you want to pay.

4) Click paid, and pay your group member back!

Groups

How do I add a group?

1) Click on the groups tab

2) Input name, description, dates, and invite members

Can I delete a group?

1) You may not delete a group but you may remove yourself from it

2) If all members of a group leave, the group will be deleted

How do I remove myself from a group?

1) Select the group you would like to leave

2) Click the “Leave Group” button in the group description
